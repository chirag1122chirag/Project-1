About CCAS Showroom


Welcome to CCAS Showroom, your premier destination for all things contemporary crafts and arts supplies. At CCAS, we are passionate about nurturing creativity and supporting artists, crafters, and hobbyists on their creative journeys. Our showroom is more than just a store; it's a haven for all those who seek inspiration, innovation, and high-quality materials to bring their artistic visions to life.

Our mission


At CCAS Showroom, our mission is simple: to be the go-to place for artists and craft enthusiasts, providing them with the finest selection of art and craft supplies. We strive to empower and inspire everyone to explore their creative potential, no matter their level of expertise or experience.

Why Choose CCAS Showroom?

  1. Diverse Product Range: We curate a wide range of products that cater to various art forms, from painting and drawing to sculpting and DIY crafts. You'll find everything you need under one roof.

  2. Quality Assurance:We are committed to offering only the highest quality supplies from reputable brands. Your creative projects deserve the best, and we're here to provide it.

  3. Expertise and Support:Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is here to assist you with product recommendations, tips, and guidance. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned artist, we're dedicated to helping you achieve your artistic goals.

  4. Community Hub:CCAS Showroom isn't just a store; it's a place to connect with other artists, attend workshops, and be part of a vibrant artistic community. We believe in the power of creativity to bring people together.
